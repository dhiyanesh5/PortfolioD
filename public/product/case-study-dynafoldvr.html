Product Concept: DynafoldVR

An immersive AI and VR platform to accelerate scientific discovery in structural biology. My B.Tech Project proposal.

Back to Portfolio

The Problem: We Study 3D Molecular Interactions on 2D Screens

The binding of proteins to DNA is a fundamentally three-dimensional process. However, our primary tools like PyMOL are flat, forcing researchers to mentally reconstruct complex docking and obscuring insights into binding motifs. This creates a cognitive barrier, slowing down the pace of discovery.

The Opportunity: A Multi-Billion Dollar Market Held Back by Outdated Tools

The global scientific visualization software market is projected to reach over $5 billion by 2030. Our initial target is the academic and biotech research segment. We identified a clear gap for a tool that offers true immersion and intuitive interaction, unlike competitors like PyMOL, ChimeraX, and VMD.

The Solution: From Observation to True Immersion

DynafoldVR is an interactive playground that replaces the mouse and keyboard with your hands and voice. Our MVP focuses on three core features:

  1. Immersive Visualization: Load and inspect PDB structures in a true 3D environment.
  2. Voice-Powered AI Assistant: Use an offline AI to load structures and control the view with natural language.
  3. Simulated Docking: A proof-of-concept for manual protein positioning with hands.

The Roadmap: From Visualization to AI-Powered Discovery

Our vision extends beyond a simple viewer. We planned a three-phase roadmap:

  • Phase 1 (MVP Launch): Deliver the core visualization and voice control features.
  • Phase 2 (Collaboration & Simulation): Introduce multi-user "virtual labs" and a basic physics engine.
  • Phase 3 (AI-Powered Discovery): Integrate AlphaFold3 for real-time feedback on binding affinity, transforming the tool into a predictive engine.
