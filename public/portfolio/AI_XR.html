AI for XR PoC

Tasked with exploring novel AI applications in Extended Reality for the Godrej Enterprises Group, I spearheaded the development of a Proof of Concept (PoC) focused on enhancing safety training within their manufacturing facilities. The solution leveraged Machine Learning models to create an effective and responsive XR training module. I successfully delivered the PoC, meeting all project expectations and demonstrating the value of AI in XR. To further increase the project's utility and cultural relevance, I integrated multi-language support, making the training accessible to a diverse workforce.
